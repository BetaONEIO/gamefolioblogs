## Formatting Your Clips for Success

To make sure your 15-second clips are truly **essential** viewing, keep these technical tips in mind:

*   **Vertical vs. Horizontal**: Many streamers browse on mobile. Consider uploading vertical clips to fit the "Reel" style of our [Latest Reels](https://app.gamefolio.com/latest-reels) page.
*   **No Fluff**: Start the clip right as the action begins. You have about 2 seconds to hook them!
*   **Visual Clarity**: Ensure your UI isn't cluttering the screen. Streamers want to see the "feel" of the game.
*   **End Card**: Use the last 2 seconds to show your game's title and a "Play now on Gamefolio" call to action.
