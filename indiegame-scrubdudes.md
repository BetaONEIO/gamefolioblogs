# Scrub Dudes - Turn a Messy Handmade World into a Cozy Masterpiece

Scrub Dudes is a relaxing cleaning adventure that invites players into a charming world crafted from cardboard, fabric, and other handmade materials. Instead of battling monsters or saving kingdoms, your goal is simple: clean up the mess, collect trash, repair structures, and restore a handcrafted world to its former beauty. 

With its cozy atmosphere and satisfying gameplay loop, Scrub Dudes offers a stress-free experience centered around exploration and restoration.

## A World Built by Hand

One of Scrub Dudes' most distinctive features is its unique visual style. Every environment is designed to look like a handcrafted creation, built from materials such as cardboard, fabric, and other arts-and-crafts-inspired textures. This gives the world a warm, personal feel that sets it apart from traditional simulation games.

## Cleaning Has Never Been This Satisfying

The heart of the experience revolves around cleaning. As a member of the Scrub Dudes team, you'll wash away mud, grime, and clutter that have accumulated throughout the world. Watching dirty areas transform into clean, vibrant spaces creates a rewarding sense of progression that encourages players to keep exploring.

Furthermore, cleaning isn't just about making things look nice and removing grime. The trash you collect serves an important purpose, allowing you to repair and restore structures throughout the environment. Every piece of debris contributes toward rebuilding the world and bringing neglected areas back to life.

## Explore Through Climbing and Gliding

Movement plays a larger role than simply walking from one cleaning task to another. Players can climb structures, glide across environments, and navigate the handcrafted world in a variety of ways. These traversal mechanics make exploration feel engaging while helping uncover new areas in need of restoration.
