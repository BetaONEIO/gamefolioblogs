## Step 3: Polish, Tag, and Title Like a Pro

You’ve uploaded the clip: now give it some soul! A great title and the right tags are the difference between 10 views and 10,000 views.

When you're filling out your clip details on [app.gamefolio.com](https://app.gamefolio.com), keep these tips in mind:

*   **Be Descriptive but Punchy:** Instead of "Goal," try "Insane 90th Minute Bicycle Kick! ⚽️"
*   **Use Relevant Tags:** This is how our discovery engine finds you. Use tags for the game title, the genre, and the type of play (e.g., #IndieDev, #FPS, #Clutch).
*   **Add Context:** Use the description to tell the story. Was this a 1-in-a-million shot? Tell us!

By adding these details, you're making it easier for our curation team to find your content when they’re looking for the next big feature. We love seeing creators who take that extra minute to make their posts shine! ✨
