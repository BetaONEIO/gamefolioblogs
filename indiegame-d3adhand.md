# D3AD HAND — A Darkly Comedic Cold War Adventure Where Saving the World Means Feeding a Cat

D3AD HAND is a point-and-click comedy adventure set in a crumbling Soviet missile bunker during the Cold War. Blending classic puzzle design with satirical storytelling, the game puts players in charge of stopping nuclear annihilation—while dealing with absurd bureaucracy, malfunctioning systems, and one very important cat.

## A Cold War Setting with a Satirical Twist

Set deep in Siberia inside the Čerëmuški Command Post, D3AD HAND places you in an alternate history where things have gone horribly wrong. You play as Yuri Spraytyvich, a newly appointed Junior Strategic Missile Force Manager tasked with keeping a decaying nuclear facility operational.

But when the automated defense system “PERIMETER” mistakenly initiates a nuclear launch, the situation spirals into chaos. What follows is a race against time filled with dark humor, political satire, and absurd scenarios.

## Classic Point-and-Click Gameplay Done Right

At its core, D3AD HAND embraces traditional point-and-click mechanics. Players explore environments, interact with objects, and solve puzzles using logic and observation. The controls are simple and intuitive, making it accessible for newcomers while still satisfying for fans of classic adventure games.

---

## Puzzles, Problems, and Pure Absurdity

The gameplay revolves around solving a variety of puzzles—ranging from mechanical fixes to problem-solving scenarios tied to the bunker’s failing systems. Along the way, you’ll deal with radiation issues, sabotage, worker strikes, and bizarre tasks that add to the game’s comedic tone.

The challenge isn’t just solving puzzles—it’s doing so while everything around you is falling apart!

## A Story That Doesn’t Take Itself Too Seriously

D3AD HAND stands out through its humor. The game leans heavily into tongue-in-cheek comedy, poking fun at Cold War tensions and bureaucratic inefficiency. Despite the high-stakes premise of preventing global destruction, the tone remains lighthearted and intentionally ridiculous.

## R3D STAR: A Full Bonus Adventure Included

Included with the game is R3D STAR, a larger and more challenging bonus adventure that expands the story. This additional content offers hours of gameplay and introduces new scenarios, including the ability to play as Stalin—the cat—using unique abilities to solve puzzles and assist Yuri.

It effectively turns D3AD HAND into a two-part experience with extended replay value.
