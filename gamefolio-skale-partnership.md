# Gamefolio Partners with SKALE on Base to Power the Future of Gaming Content

![Gamefolio x SKALE on Base](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/BetaONEIO/gamefolioblogs/main/assets/gamefolio-skale-partnership/banner.png)

Gaming content is broken.

Every day, millions of clips are created across games like Fortnite, Call of Duty, Rocket League, Overwatch, EA Sports FC, and more. Players hit incredible moments, save game winning clips, pull off insane plays, or create entertaining content for their communities, yet most of those moments disappear into group chats, temporary social feeds, or get buried by algorithms.

At Gamefolio, we believe gaming moments deserve a permanent home.

That's why we're excited to announce our partnership with SKALE on Base as we build the future of gaming identity, clip sharing, creator discovery, and digital ownership.

---

## Why We Chose SKALE on Base

From the beginning, we knew Gamefolio needed infrastructure that could scale with gamers.

Uploading gaming clips, creating social features, rewarding creators, supporting large communities, and building future digital ownership systems requires technology that is:

* Fast
* Scalable
* Low cost
* User friendly
* Invisible to the average gamer

Most gamers don't care about blockchain terminology. They care about:

* Upload speed
* Smooth experiences
* Discovery
* Ownership of their content
* Community
* Exposure

That's where SKALE on Base comes in.

By integrating SKALE on Base into Gamefolio, we're able to build a seamless experience underneath the surface without introducing friction to our users.

---

## Introducing GFT on SKALE on Base

As part of this partnership, our native token, GFT, is now live on SKALE on Base.

GFT is designed to support the Gamefolio ecosystem as we continue expanding the platform and introducing new creator focused features.

In the future, GFT will help power:

* In app purchases
* Creator rewards
* Staking systems
* Premium platform features
* Community driven functionality
* Digital ownership experiences

Our goal is to make these systems feel natural inside the Gamefolio experience while keeping the platform gaming first.

---

## The Future of NFTs on Gamefolio

We also believe gaming content deserves true ownership.

As Gamefolio evolves, SKALE on Base will help us explore:

* Minting gaming moments as NFTs
* Buying and selling NFTs
* Limited creator collectibles
* Community rewards
* Digital achievements and badges
* Tradable profile assets and cosmetics

We want to create systems where creators and gamers can truly own and showcase their achievements online.

Our goal is simple: make Gamefolio feel like the best gaming content platform possible.
