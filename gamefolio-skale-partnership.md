<p style="color: #a4e85c;"><strong>TL/DR:</strong> Gaming culture has exploded, but most gaming content still feels temporary. We're excited to announce our partnership with SKALE on Base as we build the future of gaming identity, clip sharing, creator discovery, and digital ownership — including the launch of our native token, GFT.</p>

Gaming has changed massively over the last decade. Millions of people now spend their evenings playing games, streaming to friends, creating clips, or sharing moments online. Yet despite how huge gaming culture has become, most gaming content still feels temporary. You hit an incredible play, save a funny moment with your friends, or pull off something rare in game, only for that clip to disappear into a WhatsApp chat, get buried on social media, or lost somewhere on your hard drive.

That's one of the main reasons we started building Gamefolio.

We wanted to create a place where gaming moments actually matter. A platform where gamers, streamers, indie developers, and creators can build their identity around the games they love. Somewhere your clips are stored, showcased, shared, and discovered properly.

As we continue building towards that vision, we're excited to announce our partnership with SKALE on Base.

From day one, we knew the technology powering Gamefolio had to scale with the community we want to build. Gaming content moves fast. Uploads, engagement, social features, progression systems, creator rewards, and digital ownership all require infrastructure that is fast, scalable, and user friendly.

Most importantly, it needs to stay invisible to the average gamer.

We don't believe players should need to understand blockchain terminology, gas fees, wallets, or complicated onboarding processes just to enjoy a gaming platform. The experience should feel smooth and natural. The technology should improve the platform quietly in the background without getting in the way.

That's exactly why SKALE on Base stood out to us.

By building with SKALE on Base, we're able to explore powerful new systems for Gamefolio while keeping the platform gaming first. Some of the biggest advantages SKALE on Base brings to Gamefolio include:

* Fast and scalable infrastructure for a growing gaming community
* Low cost transactions and interactions
* A smoother experience for users without blockchain friction
* Support for future creator rewards and staking systems
* Infrastructure for NFTs, digital collectibles, and ownership features
* Improved scalability for uploads, progression systems, and future marketplace features
* The ability to grow without sacrificing speed or user experience

Alongside this partnership, our native token GFT is now live on SKALE on Base.

GFT will become an important part of the Gamefolio ecosystem over time. We're designing it to support things like in app purchases, creator focused systems, staking, premium features, and future community driven functionality. The goal is not to force crypto into gaming, but to create systems that genuinely improve the experience for players and creators using the platform every day.

One area we're especially excited about is digital ownership.

Gaming clips and achievements have real value to the people who create them. Whether it's an incredible ranked play, a funny gaming moment with friends, or a rare achievement captured forever, we believe players should have more control over how those moments are stored and showcased online.

Using SKALE on Base gives us the opportunity to explore future NFT functionality within Gamefolio in a way that actually makes sense for gamers. This includes the ability to mint gaming moments as NFTs, buy and sell digital collectibles, unlock profile cosmetics, showcase achievements, and support creators through limited digital items and community rewards.

The most important thing for us is making sure these systems feel authentic to gaming culture rather than forced.

At its core, Gamefolio is still about gamers. It's about helping smaller creators get discovered. It's about giving streamers more visibility. It's about building a community around clips, games, achievements, and shared moments.

We believe gaming deserves a platform built specifically for gaming culture, and this partnership is an important step towards making that vision a reality.

We're still incredibly early in the journey, but we're excited for what's ahead and grateful to everyone supporting Gamefolio from the beginning.

This is only the start.
