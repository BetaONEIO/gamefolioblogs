# RMF’s Ragdoll Physics Experiments — A Sandbox of Chaos, Control, and Pure Physics

RMF’s Ragdoll Physics Experiments is a physics-driven sandbox focused entirely on interaction, experimentation, and emergent chaos. Rather than following a traditional genre structure, the game gives players tools, systems, and scenarios to explore how ragdoll physics behave in unpredictable and often hilarious ways!

## A True Physics Sandbox Experience

At its core, RMF’s Ragdoll Physics Experiments is about experimentation. Players are placed in controlled environments where they can manipulate ragdoll characters, objects, and forces to observe how everything reacts. There’s no strict path or linear objective—just a space designed for testing ideas and seeing what happens.

The defining feature of the game is its exaggerated ragdoll physics system. Characters don’t move with precision—instead, they react dynamically to every force, collision, and interaction. This creates moments that range from chaotic to unexpectedly realistic, depending on how systems collide.

Unlike traditional games that focus on leveling up or completing missions, RMF’s Ragdoll Physics Experiments emphasizes interaction. ## Controlled Chaos with Endless Possibilities

Because everything is physics-based, gameplay is never scripted. Situations unfold naturally depending on how players interact with the world. Small actions can lead to wildly different outcomes, making each session feel unique without needing structured challenges.

The game strikes a balance between control and unpredictability. While players can influence outcomes, they can never fully control them. This creates a constant sense of surprise, where even simple experiments can spiral into chaotic results.
