# Sports Card Shop Simulator — Build, Collect, and Profit in the Ultimate Card Trading Experience!

Sports Card Shop Simulator is a simulation and management game that lets players step into the world of sports card collecting and business. From ripping open packs to running your own store, the game blends the excitement of card hunting with the strategy of building a profitable shop.

## From Small Shop to Collector’s Empire

You begin with a humble setup—a small shop, limited stock, and big ambitions. Through smart decisions and steady growth, you’ll expand your business into a thriving card shop. Managing inventory, setting prices, and satisfying customers all play a role in your journey to success.

At the heart of the experience is the excitement of opening card packs. Each pack holds the potential for something valuable, from basic cards to rare collectibles. The anticipation of pulling a high-value card drives the gameplay, capturing the real-world thrill of card collecting.

---

## A Deep and Rewarding Collection System

The game features over 4,000 card variants from more than 50 national teams. Players can collect everything from standard cards to rare holo parallels, full art cards, signed editions, and numbered cards. Completing collections is no easy task, making every rare pull feel rewarding.

## Manage, Customize, and Grow Your Shop

Running the shop goes beyond buying and selling. You’ll design layouts, decorate your store, restock products, and manage customer interactions. As your business grows, you can expand operations, unlock better packs, and improve efficiency.

Moreover, the game features a dynamic market system where prices and demand shift based on gameplay and events. With plans to expand beyond football into other sports, as well as additional features like marketing tools and shop upgrades, the experience is designed to evolve over time—especially during its Early Access phase.
