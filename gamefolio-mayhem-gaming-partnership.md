We are excited to announce a new partnership between Gamefolio and Mayhem Gaming, one of the most supportive and passionate gaming communities in the streaming space.

Founded in June 2020 by [OfficiallyMayhem](https://www.twitch.tv/officiallymayhem), Mayhem Gaming was built around a shared love of gaming, streaming, creativity, and community. What began as a small gathering of like-minded gamers has grown into a thriving network of nearly 7,500 members, all supporting one another as they pursue their goals within the gaming industry.

While Mayhem himself prefers to remain behind the scenes, his impact on the streaming community is undeniable. Through dedication, consistency, and a genuine desire to help others succeed, he has built a [Discord community](https://t.co/cZYlIaEk7h) where streamers can connect, share ideas, collaborate, discover new opportunities, and support one another on their journey.

Known affectionately as his "cultists", the members of Mayhem Gaming have created a welcoming environment for gamers of all backgrounds. Whether you're just starting your streaming journey or looking to connect with other passionate creators, the Mayhem Gaming community has become a place where people can learn, grow, and build lasting friendships.

At Gamefolio, our mission is to help gamers, streamers, creators, and communities get discovered. We believe great content deserves a home, and great communities deserve recognition. That's why we're incredibly excited to welcome Mayhem Gaming to the Gamefolio ecosystem.

Together, we hope to create even more opportunities for streamers to showcase their content, grow their audiences, connect with new players, and discover exciting opportunities across the gaming space.

We're looking forward to seeing the Mayhem Gaming community build their Gamefolios, share their clips, showcase their streams, and continue supporting one another as they grow.

Please join us in giving a warm welcome to Mayhem Gaming and their incredible community.

Welcome to Gamefolio. ⚡️🎮

## About Mayhem Gaming

Mayhem Gaming is a gaming and streaming community founded in 2020 by [OfficiallyMayhem](https://www.twitch.tv/officiallymayhem). With nearly 7,500 members, the community focuses on helping streamers grow, connecting gamers, encouraging collaboration, and creating opportunities for creators to get discovered.
