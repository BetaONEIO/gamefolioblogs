## What Exactly is the "Social Layer"?

Think of the "Social Layer" as the digital tissue that connects your game or clip to the people who actually want to see it. Instead of treating your content like a standalone product, the Social Layer treats it as a conversation starter. It’s about building a specialized environment where **engagement isn't an afterthought: it's the foundation.**

The framework focuses on four key pillars:

*   **Visual Assets**: High-quality clips and screenshots that tell a story.
*   **Real-Time Feedback**: Immediate interaction through community channels.
*   **Discovery & Rewards**: Making sure the best content gets seen and celebrated.
*   **Technical Speed**: Ensuring the experience is fast and seamless for everyone.

By focusing on these layers, we aren't just "marketing"; we're building a home for your content where like-minded gamers can connect and cheer you on. 🚀

## Phase 1: Capturing the Moment (The Visual Layer)

Everything starts with the "wow" factor. In the Social Layer framework, your visual content is your currency. But here's the trick: instead of just posting a link to a store page, you should be sharing the **emotional core** of your game.

🎥 **Show, Don't Just Tell:**  
Whether it’s a physics-defying glitch, a stunning sunset in your open world, or a triple-kill in a frantic FPS, these moments are what stop the scroll. On our [latest clips page](https://app.gamefolio.com/latest-clips), we see every day how a 15-second high-energy reel can do more for a game's visibility than a thousand words of text.

Instead of a generic trailer, try sharing:

*   **Bloopers**: Players love seeing the "charming" mistakes that happen during development.
*   **Milestones**: Did you just finish a difficult level? Share the victory!
*   **Tutorials**: Show off a unique mechanic that makes your game **special**.

## Phase 2: Real-Time Connection (The Human Layer)

The Social Layer isn't complete without a heartbeat. This is where community comes in. We’ve found that games thrive when developers and players can talk in real-time. It turns "users" into "advocates."

💬 **The Power of Discord:**  
We're big believers in the power of a dedicated hub. Our [active Discord server](/assets/how-to-promote-your-game-using-the-social-layer-framework/inline-3.webp) is the "human layer" of Gamefolio. It’s where you can get immediate feedback on a screenshot, find teammates, or even chat directly with indie developers.

Instead of waiting weeks for a review, use the Social Layer to:

*   **Ask for opinions**: "Which character design do you like better, A or B?"
*   **Run contests**: Encourage your community to share their own clips from your game.
*   **Build hype**: Share "behind-the-scenes" snippets that aren't ready for the main stage yet.

Building a community is about togetherness. When people feel like they’re part of the journey, they’re much more likely to help you succeed! 🧑‍💻
