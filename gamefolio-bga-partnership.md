We are incredibly proud to announce that Gamefolio has officially joined the [Blockchain Game Alliance (BGA)](https://blockchaingamealliance.net/).

As a platform dedicated to helping gamers, creators, streamers, indie developers, and gaming communities grow, this marks an exciting milestone in our journey as we continue building the future of gaming.

## What is the Blockchain Game Alliance (BGA)?

The Blockchain Game Alliance is one of the leading organisations dedicated to promoting blockchain technology within the gaming industry. Since its founding, the BGA has brought together hundreds of companies, developers, infrastructure providers, gaming studios, and industry leaders who share a common goal — advancing innovation and accelerating the adoption of blockchain gaming technologies.

## Why This Matters for Gamefolio

Gamefolio was created with a simple vision: to give gamers a permanent home for their gaming achievements, clips, screenshots, livestreams, and gaming identity.

Joining the Blockchain Game Alliance places Gamefolio alongside some of the most innovative companies in gaming and Web3. It provides opportunities to collaborate with industry leaders, contribute to discussions shaping the future of gaming, and help drive adoption of technologies that empower players and creators.

As we continue developing the platform, we're exploring ways that emerging technologies can enhance the gaming experience through:

* Better creator rewards
* Digital ownership
* Community engagement
* Player recognition and progression
* New opportunities for indie game developers
* Cross-community collaboration

Being part of the BGA allows us to learn from the wider blockchain gaming ecosystem while sharing our own vision for the future of gaming communities.

We're committed to building a platform where gamers can showcase their journey, connect with others, discover new opportunities, and gain recognition for the content they create.

We're honoured to join the Blockchain Game Alliance and look forward to working alongside fellow members as we help shape the next generation of gaming.

Thank you to our community for supporting us on this journey. We couldn't have reached this milestone without you.

The future of gaming is being built today, and we're excited to be part of it.
