## 7\. Turn Viewers into Participants

Finally, the most effective hack is to stop treating your audience like "viewers" and start treating them like "players."

When you share an indie game or a screenshot, ask a question. Instead of saying "Check out my new game," try: "Which of these two character designs feels more 'heroic' to you?" By inviting people to participate in your creative process, you build a bond that generic social media simply can't replicate.

**Quick Engagement Checklist:**

*   👉 Ask for opinions on game mechanics.
*   👉 Run "mini-contests" for the best caption on a screenshot.
*   👉 Share behind-the-scenes "fails" to show the human side of development.
