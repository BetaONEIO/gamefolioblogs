## Step 1: Crafting Your "Hook" Content 🎨

Before you even think about the "Upload" button, you need to make sure your content is **essential** viewing. In the fast-paced world of digital feeds, you have about three seconds to grab someone's attention.

### Instead of a long intro, start with the action!

Many developers make the mistake of starting their clips with a slow fade-in or a logo screen. **Instead of that, start with your best mechanic.** Show us that charming art style or that high-octane combat right away.

*   **Capture the "Feel"**: Use high-quality clips that represent how the game actually plays.
*   **The Power of Screenshots**: A well-composed screenshot can tell a whole story. Look for moments of high drama or beautiful environment design.
*   **Keep it Punchy**: On Gamefolio, [Latest Clips](https://app.gamefolio.com/latest-clips) are short, snappy, and full of energy.
