# Cup Or Die — A Twisted Roguelite Where Every Shot Could Be Your Last

Cup Or Die takes the familiar party game of beer pong and transforms it into a tense, horror-infused roguelite where survival is never guaranteed. Set inside a creepy bar filled with strange opponents, bizarre items, and deadly consequences, the game challenges players to think carefully about every throw. 

What begins as a simple drinking game quickly spirals into a psychological battle where every decision can help you survive—or end your run.

## A Deadly Twist on Beer Pong

At the heart of Cup Or Die is a unique take on beer pong. Players aim and throw balls into cups scattered across the table, but each cup carries a different effect. Some provide valuable advantages, while others can sabotage your run, increase your intoxication, or place you in even greater danger. Choosing where to throw becomes just as important as landing the shot itself.

---

## Psychological Horror Meets Roguelite Chaos!

Unlike traditional sports or party games, Cup Or Die leans heavily into psychological horror. The atmosphere is unsettling, the opponents are terrifying, and the game constantly creates tension through uncertainty. Combined with roguelite progression, every run feels unpredictable as new situations, item combinations, and challenges emerge.

## Every Mistake Has Consequences

Missing a shot isn't just a minor setback. In Cup Or Die, failure means drinking. As your intoxication level rises, your ability to aim, think, and react begins to deteriorate. This creates a dangerous downward spiral where mistakes make future shots even harder, forcing players to carefully manage risk throughout their run.

## Build a Run Through Strange Items and Passive Effects

As you progress, you'll collect bizarre items and passive cards that can dramatically alter gameplay. Some provide powerful bonuses, while others introduce unexpected drawbacks. Learning how different effects interact becomes a key part of surviving longer runs and developing winning strategies.

## This Table Just Refuses to Play Fair

The game constantly changes the rules. Cups can move, disappear, shuffle positions, or gain new effects mid-run. Just when you think you've mastered the table, it finds new ways to disrupt your plans. This ever-changing battlefield ensures that no two matches feel exactly alike.

This aspect is what we believe makes Cup Or Die stand out. It blends multiple genres into a single experience, combining the tension of horror, the progression of a roguelite, the strategy of risk management, and the simple satisfaction of landing the perfect shot. The result is a game where chaos grows with every round and survival becomes increasingly difficult!
